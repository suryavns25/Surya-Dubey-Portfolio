About Me

I am a geoscientist passionate about Remote Sensing, GIS, Hydrogeology, and Environmental Geology. With a Master's degree in Geology and experience in research projects like coal mining impact assessment, glacial retreat monitoring, and hydrological modeling, I bring an interdisciplinary approach combining field geology, data analysis, and geospatial technologies.

Glacial Retreat of Gangotri Glacier

Remote sensing & GIS-based study on Himalayan glacier dynamics and climate change impacts.

Environmental Impact of Coal Mining

Dissertation research on environmental impact due to coal mining using remote sensing & GIS tools.

Hydrological Modeling (QGIS)

Independent research on watershed hydrology and groundwater management using QGIS plugins.

Urban Mapping & Land Use Studies

Book chapter contribution on urban land use/land cover dynamics using geospatial techniques.
